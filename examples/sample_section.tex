\section{Model Foundations}

Modern large language models build on the Transformer architecture, which
replaced recurrent sequence modeling with self-attention and parallel token
processing \citep{vaswani2017attention}. Scaling then exposed few-shot
behavior in large autoregressive models \citep{brown2020language}, while
instruction tuning and human feedback became central to deployment-ready
assistant systems \citep{ouyang2022training}.
